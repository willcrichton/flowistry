pub enum PlaceRelation {
    Super,
    Sub,
    Disjoint,
}
Expand description

Describes the part-whole relationship between two places.

Variants§

§

Super

A is a parent of B, e.g. foo vs. foo.1

§

Sub

A is a child of B, e.g. foo.1 vs foo

§

Disjoint

A is neither a child or parent, e.g. foo.0 vs foo.1
